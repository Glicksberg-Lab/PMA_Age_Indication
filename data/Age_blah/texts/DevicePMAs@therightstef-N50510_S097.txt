Approval for the addtion of the antibiotic ampicillin/sulbactam at concentrations of 4/2, 16/8, 32/16ug/ml to the Vitek(R) 2 Gram Negative Susceptibility Test System.  The device is indicated for use in Vitek(R) 2 Gram Negative Susceptibility Test Cards for the susceptibility testing of gram negative isolates to ampicillin/sulbactam in the range of <2/1 to >32/16ug/ml.
