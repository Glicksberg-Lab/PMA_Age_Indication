Approval for the addition of the antibiotic piperacillin at concentrations of 4, 16 and 64ug/ml to the Vitek(R) 2 Gram Negative Susceptibility Test System.  The device is indicated for use in Vitek(R) 2 Gram Negative Susceptibility Test Cards for the susceptibility testing of gram negative isolates to piperacillin in the range of <4 to >128ug/ml.
