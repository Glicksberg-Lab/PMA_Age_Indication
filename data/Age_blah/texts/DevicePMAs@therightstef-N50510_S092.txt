Approval for the addition of the antibiotic trimethoprim/sulfamethoxazole at concentrations of 0.5/9.5, 2/38 and 16/304ug/ml to the Vitek(R) 2 Gram Negative Susceptibility Test System.  The device is indicated for use in Vitek(R) 2 Gram Negative Susceptibility Test Cards for the susceptibility testing of gram negative isolates to trimethoprim/sulfamethoxazole in the range of <1/19 to >16/304ug/ml.
