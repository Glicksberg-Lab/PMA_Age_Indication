Approval for the addition of the antibiotic erythromycin at concentrations of 0.25, 0.5, and 2ug/ml to the Vitek(R) 2 Gram Positive Susceptibility Test System.  The device is indicated for use in Vitek(R) 2 Gram Positive Susceptibility Test Cards for the susceptibility testing of gram positive isolates to erythromycin in the range of <0.25 to >8ug/ml.
