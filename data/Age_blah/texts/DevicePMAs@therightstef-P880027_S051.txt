Approval for 30 and 40 mm length models of the Bonnie(TM) PTCA Catheter.  The new models will be marketed under the trade name Bonnie(TM) Monorail(R) Percutaneous Transluminal Coronary Angioplasty (PTCA) Catheters and are indicated for balloon dilatation for the stenotic portion of a coronary artery or bypass graft stenosis for the purpose of improving myocardial perfusion.
