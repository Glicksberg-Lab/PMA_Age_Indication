Approval for the addition of the antibiotic benzylpenicillin at concentrations of 0.125, 0.25, 1, 2, 8 and 64ug/ml to the Vitek(R) 2 Gram Positive Susceptibility Test System.  The device is indicated for use in Vitek(R) 2 Gram Positive Susceptibility test Cards for the susceptibility testing of gram positive organism to benzylpenicillin in the range of <0.03 to >64ug/ml.
