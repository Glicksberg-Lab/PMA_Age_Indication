Approval for the addition of the antibiotic ciprofloxacin at concentrations of 0.5, 2 and 4 ug/ml to the Vitek(R) 2 Gram Negative Susceptibility Test System.  the device, as modified, will be marketed under the trade name Vitek(R) 2 and is indicated for use in Vitek(R) 2 Gram Negative Susceptibility Test Cards for the susceptibility testing of gram negative isolates to ciprofloxacin in the range of <0.25 to >4 ug/ml.
