Approval for the addition of the antibiotic levofloxacin at concentrations of 0.5, and 8ug/ml to the Vitek(R) 2 Gram Negative Susceptibility Test System.  The device is indicated for use in Vitek(R) 2 Gram Negative Susceptibility Test Cards for the susceptibility testing of gram negative isolates to levofloxacin in the range of <0.25 to >8ug/ml.
