The device, as modified, will be marketed under the trade name Mammomat Inspiration With Tomosynthesis Option and is indicated for use with the EMPIRE reconstruction algorithm for acquisition of 2D as well as 3D digital mammography images to be used in screening and diagnosis of breast cancer. A screening exam may consist of a:- 2D image set.- 3D DBT image set and a 2D FFDM image set or a- 3D DBT image set alone.
