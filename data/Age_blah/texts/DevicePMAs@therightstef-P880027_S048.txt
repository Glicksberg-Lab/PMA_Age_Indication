Approval for a new model PTCA catheter.  This device, as modified, will be marketed under the trade name Bonnie(TM) Sliding Rail Percutaneous Transluminal Coronary Angioplasty (PTCA) Catheters and is indicated for balloon dilatation of the stenotic portion of a coronary artery or bypass graft stenosis for the purpose of improving myocardial perfusion.
