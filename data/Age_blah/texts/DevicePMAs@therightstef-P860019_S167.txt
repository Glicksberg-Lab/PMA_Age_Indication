Approval for the boston scientific scimed quantum monorail percutaneous transluminal coronary angioplasty (ptca) catheter (9, 12, 15, 18, and 20 mm lengths with balloon diameters of 2.5 - 4.0 mm in 1/4 mm increments).  The quantum monorail ptca catheter is indicated for balloon dilatation of the stenotic portion of a coronary artery or bypass graft stenosis for the purpose of improving myocardial perfusion.  The quantum monorail ptca catheter (2.5 - 4.0 mm balloon diameters) is also indicated for the post-delivery expansion of balloon expandable stents.
