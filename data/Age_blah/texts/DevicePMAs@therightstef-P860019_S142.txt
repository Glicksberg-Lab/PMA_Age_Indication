Approval to extend the product line of the SCIMED(R) NC Ranger(TM) PTCA Catheter to include a 9 mm length balloon with diameters ranger from 2.5 mm to 4.0 mm in 0.25 mm increments.  The device, as modified, will be marketed under the trade name SCIMED(R) 9 mm NC Ranger(TM) PTCA Catheter and is indicated for balloon dilatation of the stenotic portion of a coronary artery or bypass graft stenosis for the purpose of improving myocardial perfusion.
