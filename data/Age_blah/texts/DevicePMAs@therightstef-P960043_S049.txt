Approval for changes to the instructions for use.  These changes strengthen the procedural instructions for using the device and add a precaution to not use excessive force or torque to advance the device, stating that such actions may result in: "ignificant arterial damage and/or breakage of the device, which may necessitate surgical removal of the device and arterial repair."  in addition to these labeling changes, you have also indicated that the physician training protocol has been revised to further address this concern.
